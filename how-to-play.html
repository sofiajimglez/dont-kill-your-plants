How to play

Goal of the game

Do you have a reputation as a plant killer? Keep this little plant alive for 20 turns, redeem yourself and earn the title of plant lover.

Game mechanics

The game starts with a sprout that we will have to take care of throughout the four seasons (spring, summer, autumn, winter) of a year until it blooms.

To take care of the plant, we will always have three cards in our hand. Each turn, we can play or discard a card.

Seasons

Every five turns we change to another season. Each of the seasons will have different effects on the plant:

  • Spring (turns 0 to 5): each turn, the plant gains 1 health point and loses 1 or 2 space points.
  • Summer (turns 5 to 10): each turn, the plant gains 1 point of sunlight and loses 1 or 2 points of water.
  • Autumn (turns 10 to 15): each turn, the plant gains 1 water point and loses 1 or 2 sunlight points.
  • Winter (turns 15 to 20): each turn, the plant gains 1 space point and loses 1 or 2 life points.

Cards

To take care of the plant, you have different cards in your hand:

  • Caress: has no effect on the plant.
  • Fertilize: adds 2 health points.
  • Medicine: adds 1 health point and cures the fungus infection.
  • Play: subtracts 1 health point.
  • Prune: adds one space point.
  • Talk: has no effect on the plant.
  • Transplant: adds 5 space points and changes the appearance of the plant.
  • Water: adds 1 water point. If the result is greater than 10, subtract 1 life point.
  • Water+: adds 3 water points. If the result is greater than 10, subtract 1 life point.
  • Window: adds 2 points of sunlight. If the result is greater than 10, subtract 1 life point.

Random events

Each turn, there is a chance that a random event will happen:

  • Cat: the plant loses 5 life points.
  • Happy: the plant gains 2 life points.
  • Mom: the plant gains 1 water point. If the result is greater than 10, it loses 1 life point.
  • Plague: loses 1 life point each turn until the medicine card is played.

Good luck trying not to kill your plant!
